The tulip is famous all over the world for its beauty, and it is also one of the most popular plants grown in gardens. The flower is bell-shaped and has three petals and three cups. As for the number of tulip leaves: its leaves are two. Or three thick green leaves at the base of the plant, found in many regions of the world; Such as Southern Europe, Asia and Africa, but the Netherlands and Iran are the most popular countries in growing this plant.
